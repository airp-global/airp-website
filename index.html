Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AIRP Global

AIRP Global is a BSC-based utility token building payment and rewards infrastructure for airport commerce. We aim to unify fragmented airport payment systems through blockchain technology, enabling seamless transactions, integrated loyalty programs, and on-chain settlement for merchants.
The public presale allocates 15% of total supply (150M AIRP) to early supporters. Participants must complete identity verification and hold a compatible BSC wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or equivalent). Vesting schedule: 25% at TGE, remaining 75% linear vesting over 4 months.
AIRP specifically targets the airport commerce vertical with a three-layer infrastructure: User Wallet Interface for travelers, Payment Rail for merchants, and Rewards Layer for ecosystem incentives. Our focus on high-traffic airport environments with structurally fragmented systems creates unique utility and adoption potential.
All treasury funds are secured by 3/5 multi-signature wallets requiring multiple approvals for any transaction. Initial liquidity is locked for 12 months via reputable third-party locker. All vesting schedules are enforced by immutable smart contracts with no manual intervention possible.
Following the presale event, AIRP will be listed on PancakeSwap DEX immediately after TGE. Tier-2 CEX applications will begin in Q4 2026, with Tier-1 CEX preparation starting in 2027 H1. Team identity will be revealed prior to Tier-1 listings.
Cryptocurrency investments carry substantial risk. AIRP is an early-stage project and may not achieve its intended objectives. Risks include: market volatility, smart contract vulnerabilities (despite audits), regulatory changes, technology adoption timeline differences, and liquidity limitations. Participants should assume the possibility of total loss and only commit funds they can afford to lose.
