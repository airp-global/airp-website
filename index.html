Frequently Asked Questions

What is AIRP Global?

AIRP Global is a BSC-based utility token building payment and rewards infrastructure for airport commerce. We unify fragmented airport payment systems through blockchain technology, enabling seamless transactions and integrated loyalty programs.

How does the presale work?

The public presale allocates 15% of total supply (150M AIRP) to early supporters. Vesting schedule: 25% at TGE, remaining 75% linear vesting over 4 months. Requires BSC wallet and identity verification.

What wallets are supported?

We support MetaMask, Trust Wallet, WalletConnect-compatible wallets, and Binance Chain Wallet. All connections are secured through WalletConnect v2 protocol with your Project ID.

How is the project secured?

3/5 multi-signature treasury wallets, 12-month liquidity lock via reputable third-party locker, and immutable smart contract enforced vesting schedules. External audit scheduled Q2 2026.

When will tokens be listed?

PancakeSwap DEX immediately after TGE. Tier-2 CEX applications Q4 2026, Tier-1 preparation 2027 H1. Team identity revealed prior to Tier-1 listings.
