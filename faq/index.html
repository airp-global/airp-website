Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AIRP Global, the presale, wallets, and token mechanics.

What is AIRP Global?+

AIRP Global is an institutional-grade blockchain infrastructure project building a unified payment, loyalty, and settlement layer for the global airport commerce ecosystem, deployed on BNB Smart Chain (BSC) as a BEP-20 token.

When is the presale?+

The public presale launches on July 15, 2026 at 00:00 UTC. It runs for 30 days or until the 150,000,000 AIRP allocation is sold out. Register on the whitelist to be notified.

What is the presale price?+

The presale price is $0.005 per AIRP. The public listing price on PancakeSwap will be $0.008, representing a 60% discount for presale participants.

What wallets are supported?+

Any BSC-compatible wallet is supported: MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Binance Wallet, and all WalletConnect v2 compatible wallets. Make sure you're connected to the BNB Smart Chain (BSC) network.

What currencies are accepted?+

The presale accepts BNB, USDT (BEP-20), and USDC (BEP-20). Minimum purchase is $50 and maximum is $5,000 per wallet.

When will I receive my tokens?+

25% is unlocked immediately at TGE (Token Generation Event). The remaining 75% vests linearly over 4 months (~18.75% per month), enforced by immutable smart contract.

Is the smart contract audited?+

A security audit is initiated in Q2 2026 and will be completed before the CEX listing. All contracts use OpenZeppelin audited libraries, ReentrancyGuard protection, and multi-signature treasury control (3/5).

What happens to unsold tokens?+

All unsold presale tokens are permanently burned at TGE. This is a deflationary mechanism enforced by smart contract — no manual override is possible.

What is the total supply?+

The total supply is exactly 1,000,000,000 AIRP — permanently fixed. There is no mint function after deployment, no hidden inflation, and ownership is controlled via multi-signature governance.

How do I stay updated?+

Follow us on X (@airpglobal) and join the Telegram channel. All announcements including the smart contract address will be published exclusively via official channels.
