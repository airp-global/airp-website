Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before participating in the AIRP Global ecosystem.

General

AIRP Global is a BSC-based utility token building blockchain payment and rewards infrastructure for the global airport commerce sector. We unify fragmented airport payment systems — duty-free, F&B, retail, and loyalty — through a single on-chain settlement layer that benefits airports, merchants, and travelers alike.
Airports run dozens of disconnected payment systems. Travelers carry multiple loyalty cards, face high FX conversion fees, and merchants lose revenue to settlement delays. AIRP replaces this fragmentation with a single token that enables instant cross-merchant payments, unified loyalty points, and transparent on-chain settlement at under 3 seconds per transaction.
AIRP is a BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain (BSC). We chose BSC for its sub-3-second finality, negligible transaction fees (under $0.01), widespread wallet support, and the large existing DeFi/DEX ecosystem — particularly PancakeSwap for initial liquidity.

Presale

15% of total supply (150,000,000 AIRP) is allocated to early investors at $0.005/token — a 60% discount to the $0.008 listing price. Minimum buy is $50, maximum is $5,000. Accepted currencies: BNB, USDT, USDC on BSC. Vesting: 25% at TGE, remaining 75% released linearly over 4 months.
Vesting prevents sudden sell pressure after launch. Presale buyers receive 25% of their tokens immediately at TGE (Token Generation Event). The remaining 75% unlocks linearly — roughly 18.75% per month over 4 months. This protects all holders by ensuring a stable post-launch token price.
